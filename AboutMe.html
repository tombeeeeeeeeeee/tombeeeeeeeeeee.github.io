About What I Do
I'm a games programmer, I've been programming since 2019 when I started a Computer Science degree at RMIT, it wasn't for me, so I took my studies to AIE, where in 2024 I finished Games Programming.
Now I love working with linear algebra, physics, and graphics, and I get to grow and flex these skills in C++!




About What I've Done
Since 2017 I have been a part time maths and physics tutor for kids from prep to 12, every class and study session has brought such a passionate love for maths. I'm really excited to keep up the trend of maths in my life. My love for teaching was allowed to spill into programming when I started my short term position at AIE as an Events Officer, which had me helping run events, and most importantly teaching programming classes to year 10 and VCE students.
